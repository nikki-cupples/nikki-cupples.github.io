Margin, Border & Padding
September 5, 2024

Margin

A margin dictates the surrounding space of an element, controlling only the outer spacial area.

Border

Resting inbetween the margin and padding, a border frames the content, providing added detailing or style to a webpage. More specifically, a border lines the perimeter of an element.

Padding

In contrast to the aforementioned margin, padding increases the space within an element's border around it's content.
